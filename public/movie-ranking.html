The Best Movies According to Lizzie

My top 3 movies of all-time.

A Chinese Odyssey

This is my favourite comedy movie, a journey of Monkey King from a different angel, including love, betrayal, and self-discovery.

Alice in Wonderland

A fantastical journey that appeals to audiences of all ages, inviting them to embrace the wonder and magic of imagination.

The Shawshank Redemption

A timeless classic that continues to resonate with audiences, reminding us of the enduring power of hope, friendship, and the human spirit
